To put it simply, this system is a proposed alternative to passwords called Dynauth (dynamic authentication).

Instead of having 1 static secret as a password, users configure a database of 10 words (known as keys) that are associated with the numbers 1 through 10 (known as locks).

Each key has to be at leat 4 characters
